    Girls Volleyball

    Girls Volleyball is a competative sport, each team puts out six players on each side of the net. There they try to not let the ball touch the ground using only three touches. They are also trying to get the ball to land on the ground of the other team, across the net. The team that accomplishes that receives a point. They play sets that go up to 25 points. First team that wins 25 points wins the set. On varsity we play best out of five sets. If we play to the fifth set, that game is to 15 points.

    2021 Division: 2019 Record: League:
    Division I 3-5 Palomar

    Cheer

    Cheerleading is an activity in which the participants cheer for their team as a form of encouragement. It can range from chanting slogans to intense physical activity. It can be performed to motivate sports teams, to entertain the audience, or for competition. Cheerleading requires a lot of spirit. Cheering at Del Norte includes stunting, chanting, and dancing. The cheerleaders cheer for the Varsity football team and the varsity basketball team.

    Boys Water Polo

    Water polo is a competitive team sport played in water between two teams of seven players each. The game consists of four quarters in which the two teams attempt to score goals by throwing the ball into the opposing team's goal. The team with the most goals at the end of the game wins the match.

    2021 Division: 2019 Record: League:
    D1 7-5 Palomar
